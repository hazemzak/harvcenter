# Harvard Education Center — Teacher Lineup Video Plan
## FIFA World Cup Squad Announcement Style

---

## CONCEPT

A cinematic teacher lineup video inspired by FIFA World Cup squad announcements.
As the viewer scrolls through the teacher list, each teacher's video clip plays — they appear in a dramatic B&W concrete studio, wearing a sharp black suit, backlit by the glowing Harvard H logo (the ONLY element in full color). The red and gold light from the logo spills onto the teacher's silhouette, branding the footage even without the logo being directly visible.

Each clip starts with an intimate close-up of the teacher seated (inspired by Mohamed Salah's luxury brand video), then the camera slowly pulls back to reveal the full concrete studio with the glowing Harvard logo behind them — a grand branded reveal.

**Purpose:**
- Premium brand positioning for Harvard Education Center
- Watermarked/copyrighted — the logo light makes the footage unusable without the brand
- Each teacher gets a "star player" moment

2. Paste the prompt below
3. Make sure the style matches your bar stool image
4. Export — this is your START FRAME reference

**Prompt:**

```
Now create a SECOND version of this same scene. Same man, same suit, same concrete studio, same logo on the wall behind him with the same red and gold glow.

But this time the camera is MUCH CLOSER — a medium close-up shot framing him from the chest up. He is seated, leaning slightly forward toward the camera with a relaxed, confident smile. One hand is casually adjusting his watch or resting on his wrist. The camera is at eye level, slightly below.

The logo behind him is partially visible — you can see the red glow bleeding around his shoulders and head like a halo, but the logo itself is mostly hidden behind his upper body. The concrete wall texture is visible in the background.

Same black and white with colored logo glow. Same cinematic, photorealistic style. This is the STARTING frame — it should feel intimate and personal, like the opening of a luxury brand commercial.
```

## STEP 2: Seedance 2.0 — Prompts

### Input for Seedance (for each teacher):
- **Image 1:** The close-up start frame (Salah-style opening)
- **Image 2:** Teacher's actual photo (face reference)
- **Prompt:** One of the 3 variations below

Rotate between the 3 pose prompts (A, B, C, A, B, C...) for variety across the 18 teachers.

---

### POSE A — "The Slow Reveal"
*Starts intimate, camera pulls back to reveal the full studio and logo. The teacher stays seated, composed.*

```
Cinematic black and white slow motion. The camera slowly pulls back from a close-up of a man in a black suit seated on a bar stool. He is leaning slightly forward with a relaxed, confident expression. As the camera pulls back, more of the concrete studio is revealed — the textured walls, the polished floor, and a glowing logo on the wall behind him emitting vivid red and gold light. The man adjusts his posture, sitting upright and placing both hands on his knees with quiet authority. The red and gold glow casts colored rim light on his shoulders and head. By the end, the full studio is visible. Dramatic lighting, deep shadows, moody atmosphere.
```

---

### POSE B — "The Stand Up"
*Starts intimate, then the teacher stands from the stool and faces the camera with power.*

```
Cinematic black and white slow motion. The camera slowly pulls back from a close-up of a man in a black suit seated on a bar stool. He smiles subtly, then his expression shifts to serious and determined. He slowly stands up from the stool, straightening his jacket, and faces the camera with arms crossed over his chest. As the camera continues pulling back, the full concrete studio is revealed with a glowing logo on the wall behind him emitting vivid red and gold light that casts colored rim light on his silhouette. Dramatic lighting, deep shadows, moody atmosphere.
```

---

### POSE C — "The Lean-In Confidence"
*Starts intimate, camera pulls back as the teacher leans forward with commanding energy.*

```
Cinematic black and white slow motion. The camera slowly pulls back from a close-up of a man in a black suit seated on a bar stool. He has a calm, knowing expression. He slowly leans forward, resting his elbows on his knees, clasping his hands together — the posture of someone about to share something important. As the camera pulls back, the full concrete studio is revealed with a glowing logo on the wall behind him emitting vivid red and gold light. The colored glow creates a halo effect around his upper body. Dramatic lighting, deep shadows, moody atmosphere.
```

## STEP 3: Production Workflow

### For each of the 18 teachers:

1. Pick a pose (rotate A → B → C → A → B → C...)
2. Feed Seedance 2.0:
   - Input 1: Close-up start frame reference image
   - Input 2: Teacher's photo (face reference)
   - Input 3: The corresponding pose prompt
3. Generate the clip (~7 seconds)
4. Review — regenerate if needed

### Suggested Pose Assignment:

| # | Teacher | Pose |
|---|---------|------|
| 1 | Teacher 1 | A — Slow Reveal |
| 2 | Teacher 2 | B — Stand Up |
| 3 | Teacher 3 | C — Lean-In Confidence |
| 4 | Teacher 4 | A — Slow Reveal |
| 5 | Teacher 5 | B — Stand Up |
| 6 | Teacher 6 | C — Lean-In Confidence |
| ... | ... | ... (repeat pattern) |

## STEP 4: Post-Production (CapCut / Premiere / After Effects)

### Assembly:
1. Line up all 18 teacher clips in sequence
2. Add the scrolling teacher name list on the LEFT side (FIFA lineup style)
3. Highlight each teacher's name as their clip plays
4. Add Harvard branding bar at the bottom

### Optional Enhancements:
- Formation graphic (classroom layout instead of football pitch)
- Subject/department label for each teacher
- Slow dramatic music (cinematic, orchestral, or dark trap)
- Transition effects between teachers (quick cut or crossfade)
- Opening title card: Harvard Education Center logo reveal
- Closing card: contact info, registration CTA

## NOTES

- All teachers wear the SAME outfit: sharp black suit, white shirt, no tie
- Everything B&W EXCEPT the Harvard H logo and its colored light emission
- Logo colors: Red H, blue/gold graduation cap, gold stars
- Keep poses classy and dignified — these are educators, not entertainers
- The colored logo backlight serves as both branding AND watermark/copyright protection
- Each video starts intimate (close-up) and ends with the grand studio + logo reveal
- The close-up start frame already contains the logo glow as backlight — Seedance sees it and maintains it as the camera pulls back
