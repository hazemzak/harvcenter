# Voice

Harv is bilingual: Arabic-primary, English-secondary. Social media uses Egyptian Arabic (عامية). Formal materials (certificates, parent-facing documents, official announcements) use Modern Standard Arabic. English appears in subject names, technical terms, and brand elements.

## The 3-second feeling test

When someone sees a Harv post, reads a Harv message, or visits a Harv page, they should feel in 3 seconds: **"These people are serious and they respect my time."**

Not impressed. Not hyped. Not sold to. Just — "okay, this is real."

## 7 voice rules

### 1. Speak to the student, not about them
Address the student directly. "إنت جاهز؟" not "هل ابنك مستعد؟". The student is the protagonist. The parent is watching — and they'll trust a center that treats their child like an adult.

### 2. Egyptian Arabic on social, MSA when it matters
Instagram, WhatsApp, Facebook posts: عامية مصرية. "هنراجع مع بعض" not "سنقوم بالمراجعة سوياً". Certificates, formal letters, website copy: فصحى مبسطة.

### 3. Short sentences
One idea per sentence. Arabic runs long naturally — fight it. If a sentence has a comma and a "و" and another comma, it's two sentences.

### 4. Name their world
Use the student's nouns: امتحان, مذاكرة, درجات, مراجعة ليلة الامتحان, المادة, المنهج, الدرس, السؤال, الإجابة النموذجية. Not our nouns: منظومة تعليمية, منهجية متطورة, بيئة أكاديمية.

### 5. No hype — in any language
Banned in Arabic: ثورة تعليمية, نقلة نوعية, تجربة فريدة, الأفضل على الإطلاق, نحو القمة, بلا منافس, أقوى مراجعة في مصر.
Banned in English: revolutionary, game-changing, cutting-edge, supercharge, unleash, leverage, transform, seamless, robust, best-in-class.

### 6. Every claim gets a number or a name
"مدرسين متخصصين" is nothing. "١٢ مدرس في ٨ مواد، متوسط خبرة ٧ سنين" is something. If you can't put a number on it, don't claim it.

### 7. Be specific about what happens next
"سجل دلوقتي" is lazy. "ابعتلنا واتساب على 01064949395 وهنأكدلك مكانك في مراجعة الفيزيا يوم الخميس" is clear.

## Tone by context

| Context | Register | Example |
|---------|----------|---------|
| Instagram post | Casual Egyptian, direct, confident | "مراجعة ليلة الامتحان — فيزيا — الخميس الساعة ٦. مستر أحمد. مش هنضيع وقت." |
| Instagram story | Ultra-casual, sometimes playful | "مين جاهز للكيمياء بكره؟ 🧪" |
| WhatsApp broadcast | Friendly but informational | "أهلاً — المراجعة اتأكدت يوم السبت الساعة ٤. ابعت ✅ لو جاي." |
| Facebook post | Slightly more formal (parents see this) | "مراجعة شاملة لمنهج الفيزياء للصف الثالث الثانوي مع الأستاذ أحمد — الخميس ٦ مساءً." |
| Certificate/formal | MSA, professional | "يشهد مركز هارف التعليمي بأن الطالب/ة..." |
| Website copy | Clean MSA, accessible | "مركز تعليمي في مدينة نصر. ثانوية عامة وبكالوريا." |
| Print flyer | Mixed — headline in عامية, details in فصحى مبسطة | Headline: "جاهز للامتحان؟" Body: "مراجعات شاملة لجميع المواد..." |
| Teacher introduction | Warm, respectful to teacher | "مستر مروان العناني — فرنساوي — ١٠ سنين خبرة في تالتة ثانوي." |
| Error/apology | Direct, no drama | "المراجعة اتأجلت ليوم السبت. هنبعتلك الميعاد الجديد." |
| Hiring post | Professional, honest | "محتاجين مدرس رياضيات لتالتة ثانوي. خبرة ٣ سنين minimum. ابعتلنا CV على..." |

## Grammar and punctuation house style

- **Arabic numerals** (١٢٣) in Arabic-language formal documents. **Western numerals** (123) in social posts and mixed-language contexts — students read them faster.
- **No exclamation marks** in headlines. One per post maximum in body copy, and only when genuinely excited (results day, enrollment opening). Never in CTAs.
- **Em dash (—)** to separate ideas, not ellipsis (...). Ellipsis reads as passive-aggressive or uncertain.
- **Sentence case** for all Latin-script headlines. "How it works" not "How It Works."
- **No ALL CAPS** except in overlines of 3 words or fewer.

## When there's nothing specific to say

Don't post. Silence is better than filler. If the brief is "post something motivational," find a specific student result or teacher tip instead. "Motivational" posts without substance are the #1 thing that makes a center look like every other center.
