# LinkedIn — social-linkedin.md

LinkedIn is a secondary channel for Harv. Used primarily for credibility, hiring teachers, and reaching parents who work in corporate environments.

---

## Aspect ratios

| Format | Ratio | Pixels |
|--------|-------|--------|
| Single image | 1:1 | 1200 × 1200 |
| Carousel | 4:5 | 1080 × 1350 |

## Post types

### 1. Results / stats
Share pass rates, student achievements, year-over-year growth. Use the stat card layout from Instagram, adapted to 1:1. Always include specific numbers.

### 2. Teacher spotlight
Introduce a teacher: photo, name, subject, years of experience, one personal detail. Humanizes the center for a professional audience.

### 3. Hiring / recruitment
When hiring new teachers. Keep it factual: subject needed, qualifications, how to apply. No "join our amazing family" language.

### 4. Thought leadership (rare)
Only when there's something genuinely useful to say about education in Egypt. Not generic motivation. Example: "تقييم مستمر أحسن من امتحان واحد آخر السنة — وده اللي بنعمله في Harv."

## Company page

- **Profile photo:** Red H mark on white, square crop
- **Banner:** Navy background, "Harv | مركز تعليمي — مدينة نصر" in white, clean
- **Tagline:** "معاك للنهاية"
- **About:** Use the paragraph boilerplate from `voice/homepage-copy.md`

## What NOT to do

- Repost Instagram content without adapting the caption for a professional audience
- Use emojis heavily (LinkedIn audience expects restraint)
- Post more than 2x per week
- Use hashtags like #تفوق #نجاح — use #education #Cairo #تعليم instead
- Post AI-generated content or Higgsfield videos
