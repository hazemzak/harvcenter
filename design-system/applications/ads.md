# Ads — ads.md

Paid ads on Facebook and Instagram. The principle: a student scrolling their feed gives you 3 seconds. Make them count.

---

## The 3-second hallway test

Hold the ad at arm's length. Can you understand:
1. What this is about?
2. Who it's for?
3. What to do next?

If any answer is "no," simplify.

---

## Static ad sizes

| Surface | Size | Notes |
|---------|------|-------|
| Instagram feed | 1080 × 1350 (4:5) | Default for most campaigns |
| Instagram story | 1080 × 1920 (9:16) | Swipe-up or link sticker |
| Facebook feed | 1200 × 628 | Landscape format |
| Facebook story | 1080 × 1920 | Same as IG story |

## Composition — 4 elements only

Every ad contains exactly these elements. Nothing more:

1. **Headline** — Cairo Bold, 24–32px, 1 line max
2. **Subhead** — Cairo Regular, 16px, 1 line, a specific claim
3. **CTA** — Red pill button or clear WhatsApp number
4. **H mark** — Small, corner, always present

Optional: one real photo (teacher or student, background-removed). Never more than one photo per ad.

## Backgrounds

Pick ONE per ad:
- **Navy solid** (#1A2744) — default, works for everything
- **Off-white solid** (#FAFAF8) — for softer, information-heavy ads
- **Real photo background** — only if it's a real classroom/center photo, with a dark overlay (navy at 70% opacity) for text readability

Never: gradient backgrounds, patterns, stock imagery, AI-generated scenes.

## Ad copy voice

Cut 40% more words than you would on the website. Ads are shorter than homepage copy.

**Facebook ad examples:**

❌ "مستقبلك يبدأ من هنا بثقة! انضم لنخبة من أفضل المدرسين في سنتر هارفارد التعليمي واستعد لتحقيق التفوق والنجاح!"

✅ "14 مدرس متخصص. 8 مواد. 92% نسبة نجاح.
الحجز مفتوح — واتساب 01064949395."

**Instagram story ad:**

❌ Big text with emojis, gold borders, 3D logo

✅ Navy background. "الحجز مفتوح 2027" in white. Year in red. WhatsApp number. H mark. Done.

## What's never in an ad

- More than 20 words of text
- More than 1 CTA
- Gold borders or ornamental frames
- 3D logo renders
- Harvard campus imagery
- AI-generated humans
- Discount/urgency language ("آخر فرصة! عرض لفترة محدودة!")
- Multiple teacher photos competing for attention
- Emojis as text substitutes

## Campaign types

### 1. Enrollment (seasonal)
- Run: June–September (enrollment season)
- Message: "الحجز مفتوح + year + curriculum + WhatsApp"
- Format: static 4:5 feed + story

### 2. Exam review (seasonal)
- Run: 2 weeks before each exam session
- Message: "مراجعة [subject] — [date/time]"
- Format: teacher card format from Instagram templates

### 3. Results celebration (post-results)
- Run: when results come out
- Message: "92% نسبة نجاح" + specific student highlights
- Format: stat card or results post from Instagram templates

### 4. Awareness (always-on, low budget)
- Run: year-round, low spend
- Message: "Harv — مركز تعليمي في مدينة نصر"
- Format: simple 1:1 with logo + tagline + WhatsApp

## What success looks like on paid

- **Enrollment ads:** measure by WhatsApp messages received, not clicks
- **Review session ads:** measure by attendance
- **Results ads:** measure by reach and shares (brand building)
- Track with UTM parameters on WhatsApp links where possible
