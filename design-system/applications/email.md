# Email & WhatsApp — email.md

In Egypt, WhatsApp is the primary messaging channel. Email is secondary. This guide covers both.

---

## WhatsApp broadcasts (primary channel)

### Format
- Plain text. No HTML, no formatting complexity.
- Maximum 4 lines for a broadcast message.
- One clear action per message.
- Include relevant link or phone number.

### Structure
```
[What's happening — 1 line]
[Key details — date, time, subject — 1-2 lines]
[Action — 1 line]
```

### Examples

**Class reminder:**
> مراجعة فرنساوي بكرة الخميس 6:30 م مع مسيو مروان.
> المنهج كامل.
> المكان: Harv — المقر الرئيسي.

**Schedule change:**
> مراجعة الفيزياء اتأجلت من بكرة للأحد نفس الميعاد.
> عندك سؤال؟ رد على الرسالة دي.

**Enrollment:**
> الحجز لسنة 2027 فتح.
> ثانوية عامة + بكالوريا.
> للحجز: رد على الرسالة دي أو تعالى المقر.

### Rules
- No emoji walls (1 emoji max, and only if it adds clarity)
- No "أهلاً بيكم في سنتر هارفارد التعليمي" openings — get to the point
- No marketing language — this is a personal channel
- Send frequency: max 3 messages per week during peak season, 1 per week otherwise
- Always give an opt-out path: "لو مش عايز تستلم رسائل، قولنا"

---

## WhatsApp status

- Use for ephemeral announcements: "المراجعة بدأت," classroom photos
- Same design rules as Instagram stories (9:16, navy or off-white)
- Include H mark watermark (small, corner, 20% opacity)

## Email (secondary channel)

### Technical
- Width: 600px
- Font: system sans-serif fallback (Cairo won't render in most email clients)
- Background: #FAFAF8
- Text color: #1A2744
- Link color: #D42027
- Dark mode: test with inverted colors, ensure readability

### Marketing email structure
```
┌─────────────────────────────┐
│  [H mark]      Harv         │  ← Header: 60px height, white bg
├─────────────────────────────┤
│                             │
│  Subject line content       │  ← 1-3 paragraphs max
│  Key information            │
│  [CTA Button]               │  ← Red pill button, centered
│                             │
├─────────────────────────────┤
│  Harv | مركز تعليمي         │  ← Footer
│  مدينة نصر — المنطقة ٩       │
│  01064949395                │
│  إلغاء الاشتراك             │
└─────────────────────────────┘
```

### Subject line rules
- Under 40 characters (Arabic is more compact, but still keep it short)
- Specific, not clever: "جدول مراجعات يونيو" not "استعد للقمة! 🌟"
- No emoji in subject lines
- No ALL CAPS

### Voice
- Marketing emails sound like a note from a teacher, not a corporation
- First person plural: "بنقولك إن..." not "يسعدنا إبلاغكم..."
- One CTA per email

### What we never send
- Newsletter-style roundups (students don't read those)
- Promotional emails with discount urgency ("آخر فرصة!!! 🔥")
- Emails longer than 200 words
- Emails with multiple CTAs competing for attention
