# Logo Recreation Brief — Harv (Harvard Educational Center)

Use this brief to recreate/revamp the existing logo. The goal is the **same silhouette, made sleeker and more modern** — flat, clean, 2026-ready. Not a redesign. A revamp.

---

## The Logo Structure (MUST match exactly)

The logo is a **circular badge** containing a stylized red **"H" letterform** with a **graduation cap** and **5 gold stars**.

### The Circle
- Black filled circle with a **gold ring border** around it
- The gold is a warm amber tone, not bright/cheap yellow

### The H Letterform (the core mark)
- **Color: Red (#D42027)**
- The H is NOT a standard block letter — it's a **flowing, ribbon-like letterform**
- Two thick vertical strokes (left and right columns)
- The strokes **interweave at the crossbar**: the inner edges of each column curve toward and past the center, overlapping each other. The **left stroke passes IN FRONT** of the right stroke at the overlap
- Below the crossbar, each column curves outward into a **dramatic sweeping tail**:
  - The **left column's bottom curves to the LEFT** — like a comma or swoosh pointing left
  - The **right column's bottom curves to the RIGHT** — mirrored
- The tails make the bottom of the H wider than the top
- The negative space between the columns is **hourglass-shaped**: wider at top, narrow at crossbar (where they overlap), wider at bottom
- Overall the H has an organic, calligraphic, dynamic quality — like two intertwined ribbons forming the letter

### The Graduation Cap
- Sits on the **top-left area** of the H, slightly tilted (not straight)
- Classic **mortarboard** shape — a flat diamond/rhombus on top of a rectangular band
- Color: **dark navy (#1A2744)**
- **Tassel** hangs down to the **left** from the cap
- Tassel string is gold, ending with a small gold star or rounded tip

### The 5 Stars
- **5 stars** arranged in a gentle **arc above the H**, going from the cap area toward the upper-right
- Stars are **5-pointed**
- Color: **gold/warm amber (#D4A843)**
- Varying sizes: smaller at the edges, slightly larger in the center of the arc
- They follow the curve of the circle

## Style Direction

### What we WANT (the revamp):
- **Flat rendering** — clean, crisp, vector-style
- No 3D, no glossy/metallic effects, no gradients on the H
- Clean edges, smooth curves
- Feels modern, confident, premium
- Should look great as an Instagram avatar at 110px AND on a billboard
- Think: 2026 logo design, Gen Alpha compatible, sleek
- Can optionally have very subtle shading/depth to differentiate the interwoven crossbar strokes (left in front, right behind) — but keep it minimal

### What we DO NOT WANT:
- No 3D renders or glossy/chrome effects
- No bright/cheap gold — use warm amber tones
- No complex gradients
- No ornamental borders or decorative frames beyond the circle
- No extra elements (books, pens, torches, etc.)
- No text in the logo mark itself (text/wordmark is separate)
